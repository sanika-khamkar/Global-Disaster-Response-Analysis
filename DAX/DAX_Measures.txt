Global Disaster Response Analysis
DAX Measures

1. Total Disasters
COUNT('DisasterData'[Event_ID])

2. Total Casualties
SUM('DisasterData'[Casualties])

3. Total Economic Loss
SUM('DisasterData'[Economic_Loss_USD])

4. Total Aid Distributed
SUM('DisasterData'[Aid_Amount_USD])

5. Average Response Time
AVERAGE('DisasterData'[Response_Time_Hours])

6. Average Recovery Days
AVERAGE('DisasterData'[Recovery_Days])

7. Average Response Efficiency
AVERAGE('DisasterData'[Response_Efficiency_Score])

8. Total Countries Affected
DISTINCTCOUNT('DisasterData'[Country])

9. Total Disaster Types
DISTINCTCOUNT('DisasterData'[Disaster_Type])

10. Average Severity Score
AVERAGE('DisasterData'[Severity_Score])
